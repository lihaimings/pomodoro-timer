# Getting Started with Pomodoro Timer

Welcome to Pomodoro Timer! This guide will help you get started with the Pomodoro Technique.

## What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

## How to Use

1. Choose a task to work on
2. Set the timer for 25 minutes
3. Work on the task until the timer rings
4. Take a 5-minute break
5. After 4 pomodoros, take a longer 15-30 minute break

## Tips for Success

- Eliminate distractions during focus time
- Use breaks to rest, not check social media
- Track your progress to stay motivated
- Adjust timing to fit your workflow
